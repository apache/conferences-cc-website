About Airflow Contributor Days

Airflow Contributor Days is a user-focused embedded event taking place during the main Community Over Code conference that will bring together the Airflow community to focus on this workflow platform. It’s an opportunity for contributors to not only meet other contributors, but also meet the wider ASF community of contributors.
